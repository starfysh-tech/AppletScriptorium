Summary: From Prediction to PRO-Diction Models

Read the full article

  • KEY FINDING: Patient reported outcomes make oncology ML risk models more precise by adding symptom quality of life, and function data for individualized care.
  • TACTICAL WIN: Electronic PRO collection and EHR integration enable real-time physician alerts and dynamic models that anticipate toxicity and support proactive symptom management.
  • MARKET SIGNAL: Growing trial and regulatory use of validated PRO instruments signals rising demand for predictive platforms fusing patient experience with clinical and molecular datasets.
  • CONCERN: Fragmented PRO measurement standards and limited overlap with existing ML tools hinder implementation, risking inconsistent mortality predictions and inequitable deployment across health systems.
